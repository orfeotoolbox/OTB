Introduction

This system is a low-level feature extraction tool that integrates confidence based edge detection [2] and mean shift based image segmentation [1]. It was developed by the Robust Image Understanding Laboratory at Rutgers University. For information about these methods please consult the references , which are available at our website: www.caip.rutgers.edu/riul/ .
